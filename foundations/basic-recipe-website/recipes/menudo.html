Menudo

Copyright (photo) © 2016 by Yummy.ph

A Filipino favorite, easy-to-eat lunch dish. It is a common pick on karinderyas when eating out.

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
¼ cup Cooking oil
2 pcs. Potatoes (medium-sized; cube cuts)
1 pc. Carrot (medium-sized; cube cuts)
6 cloves Garlic (minced)
1 pc. Onion (minced)
250 g. Pork kasim (cut to small cubes)
250 g. Pork liempo (cut to small cubes)
250 g. Pork liver (cut to small cubes)
1 pack (250 g.) Tomato sauce
2 pcs. Pork broth cubes
1 ½ cups Water
1 tsp. Sugar
To taste Ground black pepper
2 tbsp. Raisins
1 cup Bell pepper (red & green; cubed)
As needed Paper towels

Steps

  1. Fry potatoes and carrots in hot oil until lightly browned. Remove from oil and transfer onto plate lined with paper towels. Set aside.
  2. In the same pan, throw in onions and garlic. Add pork kasim and liempo and fry until slightly brown. Add liver and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add pork broth cubes and stir to dissolve completely. Add tomato sauce, water, sugar, and black pepper. Mix well.
  4. Gently place fried potatoes and carrots in pan. Continue to cook over low heat until meat and potatoes are cooked through and sauce has thickened. Add raisins and bell peppers and cook for 2 minutes.
