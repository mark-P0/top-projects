Filipino Spaghetti

Copyright (photo) © 2022 by Meya Cortez, Jerome Jocson, Pepper

This is a well-loved dished in the Philippines by kids and adults alike. It is a common staple in any kind of occasion, especially birthday parties.

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
400 g. Spaghetti (cooked)
2 tbsp. Oil
1 pc. Onion
5 cloves Garlic
½ kg. Ground pork
2 pc. Pork broth cubes
2 tbsp. Tomato paste
500 g. Tomato sauce
½ cup Sugar
¼ tsp. Pepper
1 cup Water
To taste Salt
To taste Cheese (undocumented)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in pan over medium heat. Cook onions and garlic until light brown. Add ground pork and cook until color changes
  2. Add pork broth cubes. Stir well to dissolve before adding tomato paste and tomato sauce. Sauté for 2 minutes.
  3. Add sugar, pepper, and water. Continue to simmer over medium-low heat for 15-20 minutes until sauce has slightly thickened.
  4. Season sauce nicely with salt. Add more sugar if preferred. Pour over cooked spaghetti noodles in a big playyer and sprinkle with grated cheese.
