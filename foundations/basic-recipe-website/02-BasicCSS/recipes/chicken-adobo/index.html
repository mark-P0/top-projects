Chicken Adobo

Copyright (photo) © 2022 by Unilever Food Solutions

Adobo is a staple on Filipino dining tables. It is known for its flavorful taste despite it being relatively easy to prepare as well as having simple ingredients.

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
2 tbsp. Canola oil
6 cloves Garlic (crushed)
1 pc. Onion (sliced)
1 kg. Chicken (cut-ups)
2 tbsp. Vinegar
¼ cup Soy sauce
1 cup Water
2 pcs. Bay leaves
1 tsp. Whole black peppercorns (slightly crushed)
1 pc. Chicken broth cube
1 tsp. Brown sugar (packed)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in pan and sauté onions and garlic. Add chicken and sear on all sides.
  2. Pour in vinegar, soy sauce, and water. Add bay leaves, pepper, and chicken broth cube. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to a simmer. Continue to simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Remove chicken pieces from sauce and fry in another pan until nicely browned.
  4. Put fried chicken back into sauce. Add sugar and let simmer for another 10 minutes or until sauce has thickened. Serve warm.
